NASA Photo of the Day Generator

A react based site that pulls a picture and data from the NASA Photo of the Day API. You can continue to generate additional pictures by selecting the generate picture button at the top of the screen. Details about the picture, who took it, what the subject is, when it was taken and links to the high resolution image are listed below the picture.
